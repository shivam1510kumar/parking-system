Puzzle Parking System

Maximize capacity, minimize space.

Key Features

  • Vertical stack up to 70 m
  • Supports vehicles ≤ 2.5 ton
  • Fast retrieval (~90 sec cycle)
  • Safety sensors & automation

How It Works

  1. Car Drop-off: Driver parks in the entry bay and leaves the vehicle.
  2. Vehicle Positioning: Photo‑sensors verify correct placement.
  3. Automated Lift: Central lift moves the pallet (and car) vertically.
  4. Lateral Shift: Pallet shifts sideways into an empty storage slot.
  5. Stacking: Cars stack floor-by-floor, up to ~35 levels :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.
  6. Retrieval: Sequence reverses when the driver requests the car via touchscreen, RFID, or app.
